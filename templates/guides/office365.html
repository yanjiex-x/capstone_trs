Update Microsoft 365 (Office)

Host-by-host basis (Manually)

  1. Open any Office app.
  2. Go to Account > Update Options > Update Now. Or alternatively,
  3. Go to File > Account > Update Options > Update Now.
Note: By default, the Windows version of Microsoft Office 365, Office 2021 and Office 2019 all update automatically.
