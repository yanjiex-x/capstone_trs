Uninstall Adobe Flash Player


Host-by-host basis (Manually)

  1. Click here to download the uninstaller for Flash Player.

  4. Restart the machine and verify that the uninstallation is complete.

  1. Download the adobeflash script here and execute it in the machine's Windows PowerShell.
